Medieval Market in Terrassa

Step back in time at Terrassa's annual Medieval Market! Experience the atmosphere of a 14th-century marketplace with artisans, performers, and traditional food stalls. Watch blacksmiths at work, try your hand at archery, and enjoy live music and theater performances throughout the day. This family-friendly event brings history to life in the heart of Terrassa.
